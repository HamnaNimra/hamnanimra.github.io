About me

Hi, I'm Hamna.

By trade I am a software engineer, but I spend the rest of my time exploring, taking photos, and finding small ways to be creative. I love making things that just work and solving problems that are fun to figure out.

When I'm not doing that, you'll find me watching something move fast, pausing to notice small details, wandering somewhere new, or capturing moments through my camera. Motorsports give me a rush, quiet corners give me peace, and I love both.

I notice patterns everywhere, pay attention to nature and communities, and I pause often for reasons that usually make sense later. I get distracted by shiny things… literally or metaphorically.

I've lived in a few countries, which made me curious, adaptable, and comfortable starting fresh. I like learning as I go and asking why things work the way they do.

I share my life with a cat named Mimo. She is almost nine and fully convinced she is in charge.

This is where I share projects and ideas I care about. Some are technical, some are creative, all are honest. If you like thoughtful work with personality, and people who enjoy both speed and quiet moments, you'll feel at home here.
